Details That Aren't an Afterthought

Certificate of Authenticity

Every museum print is documented like the artwork it is: edition number (1/1), signed, dated, with paper type, ink type, and print dimensions recorded.

Behind-the-Scenes Story

A single page explaining why that particular photograph became the artwork — the moment, the light, the decision behind it.

Archival Presentation Box

Nothing arrives loose. Your book, certificate, and print details are delivered inside a handcrafted walnut or maple presentation box.

White-Glove Delivery

For local weddings, I personally deliver and help hang the finished piece — then take one final portrait of you beside it.
