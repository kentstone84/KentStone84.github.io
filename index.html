Why I Built ME

I’m Kent Stone, a U.S. Army veteran (10th Mountain Division). I live with ankylosing spondylitis and spinal stenosis. My world shrank to pain management and persistence.

I needed a tool that didn’t analyze me — it just had my back. So I built ME: a privacy-first journal that helps you catch patterns, remember the facts, and advocate for yourself when it matters most.

"I started coding on a broken laptop in Peru. ME became more than an app — it became a survival system for people like us."
