Section A — Client Confidentiality Agreement (NDA)

This Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Light & Stone, a sole proprietorship operated by Kent Stone ("Photographer"), and the couple booking a wedding photography collection ("Client"), effective as of the date of digital signature below.

A.1 Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means all photographs, video, raw image files, edited image files, and any personal, financial, or event-related information shared by the Client with the Photographer in connection with the booking, planning, and delivery of wedding photography services — including but not limited to wedding date, venue, guest list, vendor names, family details, and any communications between Client and Photographer.

A.2 Non-Disclosure

The Photographer agrees that Confidential Information will not be disclosed, published, shared, sold, licensed, or otherwise released to any third party without the Client's prior written consent, except:

  • As necessary to fulfill contracted services — for example, transmitting files to a professional fine-art print lab, framing vendor, or bindery under an equivalent confidentiality obligation.
  • As required by law, subpoena, or court order — in which case the Photographer will notify the Client in advance where legally permitted to do so.
  • With any second shooter, assistant, or editor engaged by the Photographer, each of whom is bound by this same confidentiality standard as a condition of being retained.

A.3 No Marketing Use Without Consent

The Photographer will not use any Client image for portfolio display, the Light & Stone website, social media, advertising, competitions, publications, or any other marketing or promotional purpose unless the Client has separately and affirmatively opted in via Section C below. This is a firm, standing commitment — absence of a signed release means absence of any marketing use, full stop.

A.4 Data Security

Client images and information are stored using encrypted, access-controlled digital storage, with access limited to the Photographer and any confidentiality-bound personnel described in A.2. Physical and cloud backups follow the same access restrictions.

A.5 Term

This confidentiality obligation survives indefinitely and does not expire at project completion, delivery, or termination of any other agreement between the parties.

Section B — Copyright & Usage Rights

B.1 Copyright Ownership

Kent Stone, as the creator of each photograph, retains full copyright ownership of all images captured during the Client's engagement, in accordance with the U.S. Copyright Act, 17 U.S.C. § 101 et seq. Copyright is not transferred to the Client upon delivery of images.

B.2 Client License

Upon final payment and delivery, the Client is granted a personal-use license to:

  • Print images for personal and family use, in any size or format.
  • Share images digitally with family and friends.
  • Post images to personal (non-commercial) social media accounts, with photographer credit appreciated but not required.

This license does not permit the Client to: sell or license images to any third party, use images for any commercial purpose, submit images to competitions or publications under the Client's own name, or alter, crop, filter, or otherwise materially edit the images without the Photographer's written consent.

B.3 No Marketing Use by Photographer

As stated in Section A.3, the Photographer will not use Client images for any marketing or promotional purpose without an opted-in Image Release (Section C).

Section C — Image Release (Optional — Opt-In Only)

This section is optional. By default, no images from your wedding are used for any marketing, portfolio, or promotional purpose. You may revoke a granted permission in writing at any time; revocation applies to future use only.

Section D — Client Data & Privacy Policy

D.1 Information Collected

Light & Stone collects the information necessary to plan and deliver your wedding collection: name(s), contact information, wedding date and venue, guest count, and any details you share in planning consultations. Payment information is processed through a third-party payment processor; Light & Stone does not store full payment card numbers.

D.2 How Information Is Used

Client information is used solely to plan, deliver, and communicate about your wedding collection. It is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

D.3 Applicable Law

Light & Stone is a small sole-proprietor business and is not subject to the Florida Digital Bill of Rights' comprehensive requirements, which apply only to companies with more than $1 billion in global annual revenue meeting additional statutory criteria. Nonetheless, Light & Stone voluntarily follows the privacy-protective practices described in this policy as a matter of professional standard.

D.4 Retention & Deletion

Client images are retained in secure digital archive for a minimum of one year following delivery, consistent with standard industry practice for reprint and archival requests. A Client may request deletion of personal contact information (not images subject to the archival collection) at any time by written request.
